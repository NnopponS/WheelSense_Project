# For the paper (reproducibility / availability)

Evaluation was performed with a fixed train/test split (13 train, 35 test episodes per case). Data format, prompts, and outcome tables are provided in the supplementary material (outcome folder). The evaluation protocol (feature extraction, aggregation, and metrics) is described in the Method section. This supplementary material also includes a Streamlit app to view the outcome tables and metrics; training and prediction code are not included, and hyperparameters are reported in the Method section.
